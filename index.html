Welcome to my corner of the internet! I am a PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania advised by Prof. Mayur Naik. My research interests span Machine Learning, Software Engineering and Programming Languages and aim to help improve software quality. In particular, I am interested in developing program synthesis techniques using AI techniques and leveraging them to streamline the process of finding bugs, detecting interesting code patterns, enforcing code styles, and much more.
